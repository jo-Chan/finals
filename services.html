REPAIR SERVICES

Junjie Electronics Repair Service Centre is dedicated to a single goal:

Provide the best possible service for both our products and our costumers.
FLAT RATE REPAIR
Repair price is at a flat rate cost. It covers parts, labor, performance tests necessary to repair the instrument. Repairs requiring inverter problem, backlight problem, burnt polarizer and double image are included.

NOTE : Repairs can take up to 2 weeks after receiving the returned items.

**PAYMENTS ARE NEGOTIONABLE**
REPAIR BY REPLACEMENT
Some products are classified as repair by replacement, in which case a replacement unit will be offered at a increased price. If your units replaced instead of repair, calibiration costs may be added.

NOTE : We may turn down repairs if deemed irreparable or having too much damage but alternative solution will be offered where possible.
FAST SERVICE
Your productivity depends on having tools and instruments that are accurate and in good repair. Junjie Electronics provides fast, dependable service for all tools and instruments with trained technicians.
BROKEN DEVICE
If your device breaks, don't panic. Junjie Electronics offer a huge range of mobile phone & tablet repair services.Our trained technicians will repair your mobile device, keeping you informed all the way.
